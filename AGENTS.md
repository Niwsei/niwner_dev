### **‚ò∏Ô∏è Kubernetes Deployment**
```yaml

apiVersion: apps/v1

  name: course-service
  labels:
    app: course-service
    agent: course-management

  replicas: 3
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: course-service
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: course-service
        agent: course-management
    spec:
      containers:

        image: edupro/course-service:latest
        ports:
        - containerPort: 3000
```
